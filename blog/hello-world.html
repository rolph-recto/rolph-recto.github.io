Hello World, Meet Blog

Jan 05, 2016

So I finally got a blog published. Apparently if you’re a computer science major it’s now somewhat requisite to maintain a blog or website, so alas I cave in to peer pressure. But anyway, for me the main impetus for having one of these things is to practice writing. I suppose having a public audience however scant is a good forcing function to crystallize ideas and get them down on paper. So…expect words, I suppose. I’ll be mainly writing about whatever computer science topic I’m usually interested in at the moment (a lot of Haskell for the time being!), or philosophy.

So thanks for visiting! Come back when I’ve written more.
