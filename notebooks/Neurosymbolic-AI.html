Neurosymbolic AI

Mar 02, 2024

Machine learning techniques have known pitfalls at this point: they hallucinate, they require insane amounts of data and compute, they’re hard to interpret. Even so, they’re so good that they’ve made symbolic AI mostly obsolete1. But maybe symbolic techniques can come to the rescue and help with some of these pitfalls, so we should think about. Enter “neurosymbolic” AI, which aims to combine the two. The hope is that combining ML and symbolic techniques might lead to better (read: more accurate, more sample-efficient, more generalizable) models.
